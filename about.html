About Me

I’m a User Experience Designer + Developer based in San Francisco. With deep roots in marketing and a background in web development, I’m able to design simple, effective, and end-to-end solutions that keep both the end-user and conversion goals in mind.

My career in the tech industry has been focused on customer-centric fields, from Fashion & E-commerce to Fintech. Throughout my past roles, I’ve been more involved on the email marketing side of things, but have always been immersed in concepts of the user journey, cross-functional collaboration, and ideating on different test strategies through analyzing data.

My love for design was sparked over the past couple of years through traveling the world with my significant other, who is also a designer. From gazing at awe-inspiring architecture in Barcelona to scoping out the minimalistic interiors in Copenhagen, I’ve grown to appreciate the different elements of color, typography, and shape that make up these aesthetics and hope to apply my accounts into some creative capacity.

I’m looking for a UX role that will allow me to use my unique skillset and perspective to create interactive and inspiring experiences.
